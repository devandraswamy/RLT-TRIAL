Our Story

Founded in 1995, RLT Group has grown from a small engineering firm to a leading provider of industrial automation solutions across multiple industries. Our journey has been marked by innovation, quality, and an unwavering commitment to our clients.

Today, with offices in 5 countries and over 150 employees, we continue to push the boundaries of industrial automation technology while maintaining the personal touch that has defined us since the beginning.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative automation solutions that enhance productivity, safety, and efficiency for industrial operations worldwide.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted partner for industrial automation solutions across Asia by 2025.
